Responsible Gaming

We are committed to safe and responsible play. 18+ only.

Play Responsibly

  • Set time and spending limits before you start
  • Never chase losses or play under stress
  • Keep gaming fun — not a source of income

Self‑Control Tools

Contact support to request deposit limits, time‑outs, or self‑exclusion. We will help implement controls promptly.

Warning Signs

  • Borrowing money to play
  • Neglecting work, study, or family
  • Feeling anxious or guilty about play

Get Help

If you or someone you know has a problem with gaming, please seek help:

  • GamCare
  • BeGambleAware

Contact

Email support@pokerexchange.com for responsible gaming requests.
